# FORGE

**Build What Won't Build Itself**

Version 1.0
Founder: Joe Tashjy

---

## Vision

Forge exists to help ambitious people turn ideas into reality.

Not venture-backed founders. Not Silicon Valley insiders. Not people who already
have funding, a cofounder, or a polished pitch deck.

Forge is built for the millions of people who have ideas, skills, and ambition
but lack the people, structure, accountability, or confidence needed to bring
those ideas to life.

Every day, potentially valuable products, businesses, communities, nonprofits,
and side projects never get built. Not because the ideas are bad. Not because
the creators lack intelligence.

Most ideas die because the creator is missing one or more of the following:

- Time
- Direction
- Technical expertise
- Accountability
- Confidence
- Community
- Collaborators
- Momentum

Forge exists to solve that problem.

---

## Mission

Help more people build things that otherwise would never exist.

---

## Core Belief

The future will belong to people who can build.

Artificial intelligence has dramatically lowered the barriers to creating
products, businesses, and communities. The limiting factor is no longer access
to technology. The limiting factor is execution.

Forge helps people move from "Someone should build this." to "We launched it."

---

## The Problem

Millions of ambitious people have ideas. Many have valuable skills. Many have
access to powerful AI tools. Yet very few actually create something meaningful.

Examples include:

- **The Consultant** — Has a business idea. Has industry expertise. Never starts.
- **The Engineer** — Can build almost anything. Does not know what opportunity to pursue.
- **The Marketer** — Can grow products. Cannot create products.
- **The Designer** — Can create experiences. Cannot execute alone.
- **The Explorer** — Wants to build something. Does not know where to begin.
- **The Lone Builder** — Has skills. Has ideas. Has ambition. Has no team.

---

## Why Existing Platforms Fail

- **LinkedIn** — Optimized for networking. Not building.
- **YC Co-Founder Matching** — Assumes users are already founders, that conviction already exists, and that users already know what they want to build.
- **AngelList** — Optimized for startup jobs. Not startup creation.
- **Reddit** — Useful discussion. Little accountability. No structured progress.
- **Discord** — Creates excitement. Rarely creates sustained execution.
- **Local Meetups** — Limited by geography. Low consistency. Difficult to scale relationships.

---

## Forge's Core Insight

The problem is not "I need a cofounder." The problem is "I want to build
something and don't know how to get from idea to reality."

Most people do not need networking. Most people need:

- Momentum
- Accountability
- Direction
- Validation
- Collaboration
- Progress

Forge provides all five.

## Long-Term Vision

Five years from now, a user should be able to say:

> "I had an idea after work. I joined Forge. I met people I had never met before.
> We built something together. That side project became a company."

If that story happens thousands of times, Forge will become the default place
ambitious people go to build things together.

Not a social network. Not a startup network. An operating system for creation.

---

## Forge Principles

1. **Progress Over Perfection** — Users should make progress quickly. Waiting for perfection kills ideas.
2. **Execution Over Popularity** — Reliable builders should be rewarded. Not influencers. Not content creators.
3. **Behavior Over Self-Reporting** — What users do matters more than what they claim. Behavioral signals eventually outweigh profile information.
4. **Teams Win** — The best outcomes come from complementary teams. Forge exists to help form those teams.
5. **Momentum Creates Retention** — People stay because they are making progress. Not because they are consuming content.

## North Star Metric

**Active Builds**

A project with two or more members, activity within the last seven days, and
progress against milestones. Active Builds is the primary measure of platform
health.

---

## Success Metrics

Track: Projects Started, Teams Formed, Active Builds, Products Launched, Revenue
Generated, Businesses Created.

Do Not Optimize For: DAUs, MAUs, Likes, Followers, Time Spent.

Forge is measured by outcomes, not engagement.
