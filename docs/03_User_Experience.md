# User Experience

## Core Experience Goal

Users should experience meaningful momentum within their first session.

Success is not creating a profile, uploading a photo, or completing onboarding.
Success is leaving with a roadmap, recommended collaborators, and a clear next
step.

---

## Onboarding Flow

1. **Account Creation** — Apple Sign In, Google Sign In, Email.
2. **Basic Information** — Name, Location, Timezone, Occupation, Years of Experience.
3. **Skills** — e.g. Software Engineering, Product Management, Marketing, Sales, Design, Finance, Operations, AI, Data Science. Proficiency: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert.
4. **Industries of Interest** — e.g. AI, Sports, Finance, Healthcare, Government, Education, Real Estate, Gaming.
5. **Builder Archetype** — Visionary, Builder, Operator, Designer, Seller, Marketer, Analyst.
6. **Availability** — 1-5, 5-10, 10-20, 20+ hours weekly.
7. **Personal Build Stage** — Explorer, Idea, Validation, Building, Launch, Growth, Business.
8. **Goals** — e.g. Build a Side Hustle, Launch a Startup, Meet Collaborators, Learn New Skills, Find a Cofounder, Launch First Product.
9. **Partner Preferences** — Rank importance: Reliability, Experience, Availability, Communication, Technical Skills, Leadership. Additional: Local vs Remote, Preferred Roles, Preferred Stages, Preferred Team Size.

---

## Home Screen

Purpose: provide immediate momentum.

Sections:

- **AI Recommended Next Step** — Single most important action.
- **Recommended Collaborators** — Top matches.
- **Recommended Projects** — Suggested opportunities.
- **Active Projects** — Current work.
- **Progress Summary** — Milestones and activity.

---

## User Profile

Contains: Photo, Bio, Experience, Skills, Interests, Availability, Build Stage,
Archetype, Projects, Achievements.

**Version 1:** Users begin as *Unproven Builder*. No reputation score at launch.

---

## Project Creation

Required Fields: Title, Description, Industry, Build Stage, Skills Needed, Time
Commitment, Visibility.

Visibility: Public, Members Only, Invite Only, Anonymous.

---

## Project Workspace

Every project receives: Team Members, Chat, AI Coach, Files, Milestones,
Roadmap, Progress Tracking.

---

## Project Health

Visible Status: Green = Healthy, Yellow = Needs Attention, Red = At Risk.

Internal score remains hidden.

---

## Launch Marketplace

Every launch contains: Product Description, Team Members, Build Story, Timeline,
Screenshots, Milestones, Community Feedback.

Purpose: celebrate execution and inspire future builders.
