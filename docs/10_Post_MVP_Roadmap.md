# Forge Post-MVP Roadmap

Version 1.0 · Timeline: Months 2-12

Purpose: Transform Forge from a collaboration platform into the operating system
for creation.

## Strategic Goal

The MVP validates: *Can people build together?* Post-MVP validates: *Can Forge
become the best place in the world to build together?*

---

## Phase 1 — Reputation Layer (Months 2-3)

Introduce trust, reward execution, reduce low-quality collaborators.

**Builder Reputation System:** Reliability, Execution, Collaboration, Expertise.

- Reliability Signals: Milestones completed, commitments kept, activity consistency, response time, project retention.
- Execution Signals: Projects completed, launches, deliverables, roadmap completion.
- Collaboration Signals: Team feedback, repeat collaborations, workspace activity, conflict resolution.
- Expertise Signals: Skills, project outcomes, experience, verified work.

Deliverables: Builder Reputation Dashboard, badges, reliability/execution
indicators.

---

## Phase 2 — Project Intelligence (Months 3-4)

Help teams identify problems early.

**Project Health Engine** predicts failure risk, team imbalance, missing skills,
momentum decline. Inputs: activity, milestones, messages, participation, task
completion. Deliverables: advanced health dashboard, project recommendations,
risk detection, suggested actions.

---

## Phase 3 — Collaboration Graph (Months 4-6)

Build Forge's long-term moat.

- **Builder Graph** — Who worked together, who repeatedly collaborates, who launches together.
- **Project Graph** — What was built, who built it, what happened after launch.
- **Outcome Graph** — Revenue, users, retention, growth.

Deliverables: Graph infrastructure, relationship tracking, outcome tracking,
collaboration history.

---

## Phase 4 — AI Matching Engine (Months 6-8)

Move beyond rules-based recommendations. Inputs: Builder/Project/Outcome graphs,
behavioral data, team success data. Outputs: success probability, team
recommendations, project recommendations, missing team members. Goal: predict
successful collaboration before teams form.

---

## Phase 5 — Launch Marketplace Expansion (Months 7-9)

Featured launches, trending projects, builder spotlights, launch collections,
industry categories. Long-term goal: become the Product Hunt for builders.

---

## Phase 6 — Investor & Advisor Discovery (Months 8-10)

Advisor profiles, mentor matching, investor discovery, office hours, community
experts. This phase only happens after launches exist.

---

## Phase 7 — Talent Marketplace (Months 9-12)

Project hiring, team recruiting, builder discovery, startup recruiting, contract
opportunities. Goal: enable builders to monetize reputation.

---

## Future Platform Features

- **Integrations:** GitHub, Notion, Slack, Linear, Jira, Google Drive, Figma.
- **Team Productivity:** Task boards, Kanban, sprint planning, project templates.
- **Community:** Builder groups, local chapters, events, challenges, hackathons.
- **AI Features:** AI Product Manager, AI Founder Coach, AI Market Research, AI Customer Discovery, AI Growth Advisor.
- **Enterprise:** Corporate innovation programs, university programs, accelerators, incubators, government innovation programs.

---

## Long-Term Competitive Moat

Forge's moat is data: Builder Data, Project Data, Behavior Data, Collaboration
Data, Outcome Data. Over time Forge becomes uniquely capable of answering: Who
should build together? What should they build? How should they build it? What
will maximize their chances of success? No competitor can easily replicate this
because it depends on years of collaboration and outcome history.

---

## Five-Year Vision

A user should be able to join Forge, find collaborators, build a team, launch a
product, acquire customers, generate revenue, hire team members, and raise
capital — all within the Forge ecosystem.

## Ultimate Goal

Forge becomes the default place ambitious people go to build things together.
Not a social network. Not a startup platform. Not a productivity tool. An
operating system for creation.
